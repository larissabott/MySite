MY jouRNeY

Originally I took the traditional college route. My partner, Colin, and I dogsat and really enjoyed doing so during our studies. During my freetime I enjoyed cooking and making music. Once I was near finishing my general liberal arts classes and it was time for me to declare a major I felt I wasn't any closer to finding a passion I could have a career from- and lets be honest, that wouldn't set me back at least a few grand in debt. After much consideration I decided I needed to take a step back and really discover myself and how I wanted to make my mark. Being native to Brazil and living in the midwest practically all my life I was sick of the cold and figured my life in the warm no longer needed to wait. With this I decided to move to the south and had a plan to attend college there after establishing recidency. Colin, our now 1y-o white german shepherd-wolf, and I roadtripped for a couple months. I grew closer with myself and the world around me and had experiences I wouldn't have had in the college life. One major issue with the south, that was impossible to ignore, was the amount of strays~ it was heartbreakng. Everytime i'd go out there were a few id try to stop and help, many of our days were spent this way. Many were scared due to peoples past behaviour but Colin, our pup, and I did manage to help a few~ taking roadtrips back up north to family member who could give them a good home. While on 10hr+ road trips we'd dicover and listen to music, visit national parks, and read space and science articles, like the works of Stephen Hawking, Einstein, Elon Musk and eveything in between. We settled down and traveled around the south for a bit, exploring interests and running a small house and dog sitting buissness. I came to know just how important spending everyday with the ones I love was to me. I also realized not everyone needs to take the college route, with enough self discipline, passion, and training one can succed at whatever they put their energy towards. I found great interest in coding and design and the creativity and freedom that comes with it. I felt these would only compliment my other interests. With COVID surge and the booming bootcamp market it was a great time to learn the skills so I enrolled in corses and bootcamps and I took off from there!
